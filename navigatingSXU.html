Explore SXU

Within this navigation guide, you will become familiar with campus by discovering more about the various buildings and school offices. You can review this guide anytime and anywhere you want!

Whether you're a commuter, resident student or prospective student wanting to visit our beautiful campus, there's a variety of resources available to help you make your way to and around campus. If you ever find yourself getting lost around campus, don't forget to open up the SXU Campus Map for further assistance!

SXU Virtual Campus Tour

Warde Academic Center

The Warde Academic Center is where most of your classes will take place. We recommend that you find your classrooms before classes start so that you can arrive on time for class. Your school schedule will have the room number which will help locate your classroom. For more help finding your classes, utilize the Warde Academic Center Map to get to know how The Warde Academic Center is organized.

At the Warde Academic Center, you can also find many offices that can assist you. To learn more about those services, please go to the "Self-Service" tab in the navigation bar.

SXU Library

The library on campus is here to assist you throughout your academic journey by providing you information assistance and resources! You can check out the Library Homepage to search for books, academic journals, and more. As always, do not hesitate to ask a librarian for assistance.

To learn more about the library's facilities Click Here.

Bookstore

The campus bookstore is located at the south side of campus connected to the Shannon Center. Here you can purchase textbooks, school supplies, and souvenirs! Click the Online Bookstore Link to login and find out what textbooks and supplies are needed for your registered courses. This way you are one step closer to being prepared for class.
