Registering For Classes

Hey there Cougars! Registration is open until February or March for the fall semester and in October for the spring semester and summer term. All currently enrolled students may register through Self-Service menu. For students, Self-Service is the personal management tool used to review academic planning and progress, search for classes, register for classes, make payments and check grades.

Students with special circumstances may seek assistance in the Office of Records and Registration

Step 1

First, log into SXU's Self-Service webpage with your username and password. Please go to the Self-Service Menu at the bottom left side of the screen. Click "Registration" located in the "Self-Service Menu".

Step 2

Next, you will see the Student Planning page. Please click on the "Go to Plan & Schedule" link to start searching for available classes, planning classes into your schedule, and registering for classes.

If you want to see how your classes have fulfilled your degree requirements or make sure you are picking the right classes to complete your degree requirements, click the "Go to My Progress" link.

Step 3

After clicking "Go to Plan & Schedule", use the search bar to search for a class name and number.

Step 4

You can go through the filter menu on the left side of the screen to pick out the type of classes you favor.

Step 5

A list of available classes will appear and you can pick your preferred dates and times for the class. When you decide which class you want to take, click "Add Section to Plan". Then, you can click the link that says "Back to Plan & Schedule" at the top left hand side of the screen to see how it fits with your schedule.

Step 6

You will also be brought back to see the courses you planned for the semester. Click "Register Now" to officially register for your classes.

Congratulations you now know how to register for classes!
