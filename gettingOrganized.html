Getting Organized

Hello Cougars! Time-management is a great skill to have during your first year and the rest of your years at college. Your schedule may be filled with classes, club meetings, and social events, and it is important to know your priorities and manage your time. Here you will find resources that will help you organize your time at SXU by learning about helpful planners, knowing dining hours and the shuttle bus schedule, and planning ahead for school organization meetings and school events!

Recommended Planners

Canvas Student Mobile App/Canvas by Instructure

SXU uses Canvas to allow you to see their current grades, receive notifications, access course resources and assignments, submit assignments, and more. Canvas also has a to do list available for you to see to remind them of course assignments, projects, etc.. Moreover, Canvas has a personalized schedule for you to keep track of your classes and assignments. To install the Canvas Student Mobile App, go to your app store and search "Canvas Student".

myHomework Student Planner

myHomework Student Planner is a free app to help you stay on top of assignments, tests, presentations, projects, etc. by making sure you turn everything in on time. You can set reminders to assure that you don't forget to turn in something by the due date. You can also type in your current classes and organize your assignments for each class. For more information about the myHomework app check out the myHomework app webpage.

or type in "myHomework Student Planner" in your app store to install the app.

Microsoft To Do

Microsoft To Do is a free app that helps you organize your daily tasks and school work by allowing you to create reminders and lists. It's simple and easy to use. To learn more and to install the app, go to Microsoft To Do website.

Dining Hours

The Country House Diner is open Monday through Friday from 7:30 a.m. to 7:00 p.m. and on Saturday and Sunday from 10:00 a.m. to 6:30 p.m.. You can go to the Dining On Campus page to check out The Country House's Weekly Lunch Special and also see what other dining options are available on campus including their hours.

Shuttle Bus Schedule

Getting familiar with the bus schedule will be very helpful to make sure you will be on time for your classes. On the weekdays, the shuttle bus is available to pick you up at specific times from 7:30 a.m. to 10:30 p.m.. To view the shuttle bus schedule and it's specific times for the week and weekends, the shuttle bus routes, and shuttle bus rules, please visit the Shuttle Bus Schedule webpage

Fitting School Organizations/Events Into Your Schedule

You may be interested in joining a club on campus and might want to know how the club meeting times will fit in your schedule. You can go to The Den to view descriptions of each organization as well as meeting dates and times when you click "Find Organizations". You can also contact the Student Organization's president to get more information about when the club meets if it is not stated in the organization's description.

Click "Attend Events" to learn more about school events you would like to attend and RSVP. You can also contact the Student Organization presidents to get more information about when the club meets. Don't forget to add your meeting and event times to your schedule!
